Takšen ukrep izvajajo na Floridi, New Yorku in Californii.   Ali bo zato potrebna neka evidenca oseb dolžnikov, jih naj vodi zato prisotojni sodnik, v povezavi s policijo. Sodnik pa naj obvesti pristojni CSD oz. izda stranki neko verodostojno listino, da se izognemo nepotrebne birokracije.     (primer amerike - As of 2010, any parent who is delinquent on his child support payments may not receive a new passport to leave the United States. To be able to travel again, he must pay all back child support owed. As of 2010, three states--Florida, New York and California--report delinquent payers to their state departments, which then revoke the delinquent payers' passports. There is currently no law stopping parents from other countries from entering the United States when they owe child support.)   V upanju na ugodno rešitev se Vam zahvaljujemo, S pozdravi.
